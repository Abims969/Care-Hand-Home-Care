Welcome to

Caring Hands
Home-Care Agency LLC

Getting appropriate care for your loved one can be a tough
process for the entire family. With Caring Hands Home-Care
Agency LLC, you can receive in-home supportive care
services to ensure your loved ones make the most out of their
everyday lives. We are a home care agency in Indian Trail,
North Carolina, assisting all your needs through customized
and compassionate patient care.

Give us a call anytime and experience our difference in care.
